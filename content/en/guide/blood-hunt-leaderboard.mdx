Not every hero excels in the chaotic environment of the Blood Hunt. The current meta favors heroes with high survivability and area-of-effect (AoE) potential. ## FAQ

**Q: How can I check my current rank on the blood hunt leaderboard?**

A: You can view your standing directly within the game's competitive menu. The leaderboard updates in real-time, displaying the top players globally and within your specific region.

**Q: Is it better to play solo or in a squad for the leaderboard?**

A: Both offer different challenges. Solo play requires high self-sustainability, while squad play allows for specialized team compositions. Most world records are currently set by players who have mastered solo survival mechanics.

**Q: What is the most important stat for climbing the leaderboard?**

A: While total kills are important, "Wave Progression" is the primary metric for your rank. Focusing on survival and efficient clearing is more important than chasing individual kills if it puts your run at risk.

**Q: How often does the leaderboard reset?**

A: The leaderboard typically resets at the start of each new competitive season, allowing for fresh competition and new meta strategies to emerge.
