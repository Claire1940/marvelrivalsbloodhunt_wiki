## Build Systems That Directly Affect Reward Efficiency

The PvE mode is packed with systems: hero levels, trait trees, Arcana, item rarities, and forge outcomes. If you ignore one layer, your reward-per-hour drops.

### 1) Gear Slots and Rarity Priority

Each hero uses four major categories: weapon, armor, accessory, and exclusive. Rarity matters not only for base stats but also for extra effects.

| Gear Factor | What to Check | Farming Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Item level requirement** | Can your hero equip it now? | Prevents dead inventory time |
| **Rarity tier** | Number/strength of extra effects | Big jump in output/survival |
| **Ability synergy icon** | Which skill is buffed | Determines real DPS value |
| **Slot balance** | Weakest of 4 categories | Best place to patch first |

For **blood hunt marvel rivals rewards**, this means you should stop equating “higher number” with “better item.” A lower-level piece with perfect synergy can carry harder phases more reliably.

### 2) Traits: Your Hidden Carry System

Traits are often the largest difference between a stuck account and a climbing account. Each hero gets points up to level cap and can modify ability behavior, not just raw stats.

Use this priority order:

1. Core survival node(s) for your role  
2. Main damage path for your best clearing skill  
3. Free side-modifiers that reshape ability function  
4. Boss-focused adjustments before Nightmare pushes  

When players ask why their **blood hunt marvel rivals rewards** feel slow, trait misallocation is a top cause.

### 3) Arcana and Arcane Realm

There are two Arcana layers:
- Account-level Arcane Realm progression (persistent)
- In-run Arcana choices (random options each phase)

Persistent growth adds consistency across heroes, while in-run picks decide whether you spike early or struggle.

> **Warning:** Don’t force a preplanned Arcana setup if your in-run options don’t support it. Adapt to offered cards for better clear reliability.

## Smart Forge and Smelting Strategy (Avoid Wasting Uru Shards)

Most players lose efficiency in inventory management. Smelt discipline is key if your goal is maximizing **blood hunt marvel rivals rewards** over time.

| Action | Good Practice | Common Mistake |
|---|---|---|
| **Smelting** | Filter by low-value duplicates | Smelting niche synergy pieces too early |
| **Locking gear** | Lock potential build-defining items | Forgetting to lock and losing rare effects |
| **Forging target** | Select slot + hero intentionally | Random forging without need mapping |
| **Rarity expectations** | Respect RNG rates | Overspending shards chasing one perfect roll |

A clean method:
- Keep one “best current” piece per slot
- Keep one “experimental synergy” piece per slot
- Smelt the rest by filter rules
- Forge only for your weakest slot or boss-blocking weakness

This method turns scattered drops into practical progression and better **blood hunt marvel rivals rewards** output per session.
