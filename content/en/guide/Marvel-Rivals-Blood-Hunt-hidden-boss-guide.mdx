## Step-by-Step Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Hidden Boss Guide

Unlocking Kingpin requires you to play on **Extreme Difficulty**. You cannot trigger the hidden objectives on lower settings. Throughout the level, you must collect three hidden keys by completing timed challenges and environmental puzzles.

### Key 1: The Bridge Barrier
Once you reach the bridge stage, a hidden objective will appear at the top of your UI. You must destroy the barrier at the far end of the bridge and reach the subsequent checkpoint circle within a strict time limit. 

### Key 2: The Hidden Red Bushes
After the bridge, you will enter an arena area. You must locate and destroy several **hidden red bushes** scattered around the perimeter. These can be difficult to spot amidst the red-tinted environment, so have your team spread out to cover the arena quickly.

### Key 3: The Crystal Siege
In front of the castle, you will face a final timed objective. Large crystals will spawn that you must destroy immediately. While vampires will swarm your position, ignore them as much as possible and focus all fire on the crystals to beat the clock and earn the final key.

## Standard Boss Mechanics

Before you can face the hidden boss, you must defeat the standard guardians of the Blood Hunt. Each has a "gimmick" that can wipe an unprepared team.

### Capwolf
Capwolf serves as the first major hurdle. His primary mechanic involves a telegraphed dash marked by a **long red line**.
*   **The Dodge:** You must dodge his dash twice in a row.
*   **The Stun:** If both dashes are successfully avoided, Capwolf becomes stunned, allowing for a high-damage window.

### Ratatoskr
The squirrel boss, Ratatoskr, tests your team coordination. 
*   **The Red Circle:** When a player is targeted with a large red circle, **do not run away**.
*   **Damage Splitting:** All players must stack inside the circle to share the damage. If a player takes this hit solo, they will likely be one-shot.

### Dracula
Dracula is a battle of attrition. His fight is notoriously difficult due to the red-on-red visual effects.
*   **Phase 1:** Avoid his frontal cone attack, as it heals him significantly if it hits a hero.
*   **Phase 2:** You must destroy **five floating swords**. Monitor the progress bar at the top of the health bar.
*   **Phase 3:** When Dracula retreats to his throne, destroy the **two red crystals** behind him. Once destroyed, a light will appear in the center of the room. Entering this light allows you to execute him.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Dracula's second phase is extremely chaotic. If your team is struggling, consider using resurrection tokens to maintain momentum, as the boss heals rapidly if the fight drags on too long.

## Confronting Kingpin: The Secret Boss

If you have collected all three keys and defeated Dracula, you will be teleported to a secret arena to face Kingpin. This fight is divided into two high-stakes phases that require both mechanical skill and raw power.

### Phase 1: The Quick Time Event
Kingpin will frequently use a stun attack that initiates a Quick Time Event (QTE). 
*   **PC Players:** Spam the **WASD** keys to break free.
*   **Console Players:** Use the movement joystick to struggle out of the stun.
*   **The Follow-up:** Immediately after breaking the stun, dodge laterally. Kingpin follows the stun with a high-damage laser that can end your run instantly.

### Phase 2: The DPS Check
In his second phase, Kingpin moves to the center of the arena and begins channeling a massive bubble. This is a pure **damage check**.
*   **The Strategy:** Stay inside the circle and use every ultimate and ability available. 
*   **The Outcome:** If you break the channel, Kingpin is stunned for a long duration. If you fail, he releases a nuke that wipes the entire party.

## Gear Progression and Crafting

To succeed in the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt hidden boss guide** challenges, your gear must be up to par. The progression system revolves around "Smelting" and "Forging" using a currency called **Ewer Shards**.

> **💡 Tip:** Do not spend your Ewer Shards on crafting until you reach **Level 60**. This is the level cap where Legendary items become common. Crafting earlier is a waste of resources as you will quickly outgrow the gear.

If you hit a wall in the Nightmare levels, return to a level you can comfortably clear (usually 5-10 levels lower) to farm experience and better gear. Progression in Nightmare mode moves in increments of five; for example, clearing Nightmare 100 unlocks Nightmare 105.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the secret Kingpin boss in Marvel Rivals?**

A: You must play on Extreme difficulty and complete three hidden objectives: the Bridge Barrier timed run, destroying the hidden red bushes in the arena, and the Crystal Siege at the castle front. After defeating Dracula with these keys, you will face Kingpin.

**Q: What is the best build for Thor in Blood Hunt?**

A: The **Rune Awakening** build is currently the top-performing setup for Thor. It focuses on maximizing lightning damage and survivability, which is essential for clearing high-level Nightmare stages.

**Q: Can I solo the Kingpin boss encounter?**

A: While possible, it is significantly harder. Mechanics like Ratatoskr’s damage-splitting circle and Kingpin’s Phase 2 DPS check are designed for a full squad. If playing solo, ensure your gear is at least Level 60 Legendary status.

**Q: What happens if I fail the hidden objectives?**

A: If you fail a timed objective or miss a key, you will finish the level by fighting Dracula normally, but you will not be teleported to the Kingpin secret boss. You will need to restart the mission on Extreme difficulty to try again.
