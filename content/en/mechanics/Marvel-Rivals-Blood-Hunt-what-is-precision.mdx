During the final assault on Dracula’s Castle, players must destroy "Blood Crystals" to break the dark seals. These crystals do not have traditional "heads," but they do have specific "Fracture Points" that react to the Precision stat.

Landing hits on this spot with a high Precision stat will cause the crystal to shatter much faster, preventing the room from filling with lethal vampire mist. ## FAQ

**Q: Does Precision affect healing or shields in Blood Hunt?**

A: No, Precision is strictly an offensive stat in the Blood Hunt mode. It only affects the damage multipliers for critical hits and weak point strikes. For better healing, you should look into the "Potency" or "Spirit" stats.

**Q: Can I see my current Precision stat during a match?**

A: Yes, you can open the "Tactical Overview" menu (TAB on PC, View button on Xbox, Touchpad on PS5) to see your current stat totals, including exactly **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt what is precision** and how many stacks you have accumulated.

**Q: Is there a cap on how much Precision I can have?**

A: While there is no hard "cap," there are diminishing returns after reaching a 4.0x multiplier. Most players find that balancing Precision with "Haste" (Attack Speed) yields the best results for total damage output in 2026.

**Q: Does Precision work on all enemies?**

A: It works on any enemy with a designated Weak Point. Most vampires, Taskmaster, and Dracula have clear Weak Points. However, some environmental objects and shielded enemies may not be affected by Precision until their shields are depleted.
