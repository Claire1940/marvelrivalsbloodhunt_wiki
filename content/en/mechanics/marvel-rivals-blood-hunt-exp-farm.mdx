## marvel rivals blood hunt exp farm Route for Your First Hero

## Gear, Forge, and Resource Timing (The Hidden XP Multiplier)

Many players run good routes with bad crafting timing. That causes hidden losses in clear speed.

A better approach is to align forging with level breakpoints where craft quality rises. If you’re close to a threshold, it can be smarter to do one more run, hit the level, then spend resources on the stronger craft tier.

### Efficient forge cycle

| Step | Action | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Finish run and review drops | Identify weak/obsolete pieces |
| **2** | Scrap non-essential gear | Recover crafting value |
| **3** | Check if next level bracket is near | Prevent inefficient crafts |
| **4** | Craft at new threshold | Immediate power spike |
| **5** | Re-enter at target difficulty | Better clear consistency |

This loop is a major reason experienced players reach high Nightmare tiers much faster in 2026.

### When to lower difficulty briefly

You should temporarily drop a tier when:

- You need 1 level to hit a craft breakpoint
- Your deaths are causing repeated run collapses
- Team composition is unstable in queue

Lowering once for stability can be better than chain-failing at a tier that is currently too slow or inconsistent.

## Alt Hero Leveling: Carry Strategy That Saves Hours

This is where the **marvel rivals blood hunt exp farm** process becomes dramatically faster. Once your account has unlocked higher tiers, alternate heroes can gain huge XP by joining a stronger teammate in content they couldn’t normally clear alone at level 1.

The principle is simple: your alt hero receives XP and class-appropriate gear from the completed run, even with minimal contribution.

### How to run it cleanly

1. Unlock a meaningful Blood Hunt/Nightmare tier on your main progression path
2. Switch to your low-level alt hero
3. Party with a friend who can comfortably clear that selected tier
4. Complete the run (the friend handles most combat load)
5. Spend points, equip new gear, repeat at appropriate tier

| Carry Setup Element | Best Practice | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier choice** | Pick a tier your carrier can clear comfortably | Reduces wipe risk |
| **Party size** | Duo is often easiest to coordinate | Faster queue and pacing |
| **Alt hero role** | Stay alive, assist where possible | Keeps momentum stable |
| **Post-run actions** | Allocate points + equip upgrades immediately | Maximizes next run value |

> **Tip:** Coordinate mutual carries. Rotating boosts between friends keeps progression fair and sustainable.

This method is often described as an “instant” leveling shortcut, but in practice it still requires solid tier unlocks, social coordination, and repeat runs. It is fast, not magical.
