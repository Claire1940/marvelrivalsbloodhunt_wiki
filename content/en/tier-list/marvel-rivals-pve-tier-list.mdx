## The Undisputed S-Tier: Squirrel Girl and Moon Knight

As of the latest 2026 updates, Squirrel Girl and Moon Knight have solidified their spots at the top of the **marvel rivals pve tier list**. While Moon Knight provides the "raw power" needed to melt boss health bars in seconds, Squirrel Girl is widely considered the most versatile hero for carrying uncoordinated teams.

### Squirrel Girl: The Queen of Blood Hunt
Squirrel Girl’s strength lies in her dual-path trait tree. Players can invest in the "Gold" side to enhance her Burst Acorns (primary attack) or the "Blue" side to maximize her "Squirrel Friends." These furry companions attach to enemies, dealing continuous bite damage while applying Slow and Vulnerability debuffs.

The secret to her S-tier status is the **Mammal Bond** mechanic. When built correctly with traits like *Natural Symbiosis* and *Rhythm of Nature*, Squirrel Girl can stay in her bonded form almost indefinitely. This allows her to spam Tail Jumps for infinite flight and use Squirrel Blockade for constant crowd control.

### Moon Knight: The Boss Killer
If your goal is purely to see the highest damage numbers possible, Moon Knight is your go-to choice. His kit in PvE scales aggressively with precision stats, allowing his projectiles to crit for astronomical values. He lacks the self-healing and utility of Squirrel Girl, but in a coordinated team with a dedicated Strategist, his DPS is unmatched.

To maximize your efficiency on the **marvel rivals pve tier list**, you must optimize your skill tree. You will not have enough points to max out every node, so prioritizing the right stats is vital.

| Tree Category | Recommended Nodes | Priority |

| **Purple (Crit)** | Precision Rate, Precision Damage | **Critical** |
| **Gold (Combat)** | Burst Acorn Enhancements | **Medium** |

Focusing on the Purple tree first is generally the best strategy for PvE. While it may be tempting to put points into Health or Armor, expert players suggest relying on legendary gear for survivability and using your skill points to maximize damage output.

## Gear Progression and the Forge System

Reaching level 60 is just the beginning of your journey. To truly excel at the high-end challenges listed in the **marvel rivals pve tier list**, you need to engage with the Forge and Arcana systems.

1.  **Upgrade Arcana Early:** This increases your overall drop rate, ensuring you find more legendary items during your runs.
2.  **Scrap to Craft:** Don't hoard low-level gear. Use the scrap feature to gain shards, which are necessary for crafting at the Forge once you hit level 60.
3.  **Hunt for Precision:** For Duelists like Squirrel Girl, look for gear that offers "Precision Damage" and "Output Boost Damage."
4.  **Special Enhancements:** Look for specific gear perks that extend ability durations, such as increasing the time spent in Mammal Bond.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not spend your rare crafting shards on gear before level 60. The stat difference between level 50 and level 60 legendaries is significant enough to make early crafting a waste of resources.

## Team Synergy and Positioning

Even the best heroes on the **marvel rivals pve tier list** can struggle if they are poorly positioned. In Blood Hunt, bosses often have "one-shot" mechanics that require quick reactions.

*   **Vanguards (Tanks):** Should focus on drawing aggro and grouping mobs together for Duelists to AoE down.
*   **Duelists (DPS):** Must maintain a safe distance. For Squirrel Girl, this means staying in the air using infinite Tail Jumps. For Moon Knight, it means utilizing cover and high ground.
*   **Strategists (Healers):** Should prioritize heroes who are currently "drawing fire" and keep debuffs cleared from the team.

Optimizing for the **marvel rivals pve tier list** requires a balance of self-sufficiency and team contribution. Squirrel Girl is a favorite because she provides her own healing via health pack drops during Mammal Bond, taking the pressure off the team's Strategist.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best character for soloing PvE content?**

A: Currently, Squirrel Girl is the best for solo play. Her ability to stay airborne, heal herself, and crowd-control large groups of enemies makes her incredibly self-sufficient compared to other heroes.

**Q: How often does the marvel rivals pve tier list change?**

A: The tier list typically shifts with every major seasonal update or when new heroes are introduced. However, core mechanics like Precision scaling have remained consistent throughout 2026.

**Q: Is it worth playing Vanguard heroes in PvE?**

A: Yes. While Duelists deal the most damage, Vanguards like Hulk or Magneto are essential for higher-difficulty "Horde" stages where you can easily be overwhelmed by sheer numbers.

**Q: What is the most important stat for late-game PvE?**

A: Precision Damage is widely considered the most important stat for late-game. As you reach level 60, your ability to land critical hits will determine whether you can clear boss encounters before they enter their "enrage" timers.
