## Team Synergies and Counters

No **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Jeff build** exists in a vacuum. To maximize your effectiveness, you need to coordinate with your team. Jeff excels when paired with high-health Vanguards who can take the brunt of the damage while he provides splash healing.

### Best Partners for Jeff
*   **Venom:** Jeff can hide under Venom’s massive frame, making it nearly impossible for snipers to pick the shark off.
*   **Groot:** Groot’s walls can trap enemies in small spaces, making Jeff’s "Joyful Splash" and "Frozen Bubble" incredibly easy to land.
*   **Doctor Strange:** Strange provides the verticality Jeff lacks. Portals can help Jeff reach high-ground positions to rain down healing.

### How to Counter Jeff
If you find yourself playing against a **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Jeff build**, you need to focus on displacement and "Anti-Heal" mechanics. Heroes like **Magneto** can pull Jeff out of his submerged state, while **Iron Man** can target him from the air where Jeff's water projectiles have a harder time reaching.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never chase a submerged Jeff into a narrow corridor. He is likely leading you into a Frozen Bubble trap or toward his teammates.
