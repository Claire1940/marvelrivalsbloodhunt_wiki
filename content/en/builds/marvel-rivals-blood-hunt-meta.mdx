## Solo vs Team Meta: How to Adjust Without Rebuilding Everything

A lot of players assume solo and team play require entirely different loadouts. In reality, the **marvel rivals blood hunt meta** lets you keep one core setup and swap a few traits or utility picks.

| Mode | Main Requirement | Recommended Adjustment |
|---|---|---|
| **Solo** | Boss kill certainty | Keep highest single-target loop active at all times |
| **Duo/Trio** | Role clarity and pace | One boss shredder + one wave clearer + one stabilizer |
| **Carry Runs** | Error tolerance | Slightly more health/sustain to protect run tempo |

### Team composition template

1. **Primary boss killer** (Punisher/Thor/Moon Knight style setup)  
2. **Wave control damage** (Squirrel Girl or hybrid Moon Knight)  
3. **Safety utility / sustain anchor** (Jeff-oriented support pressure)

> **Warning:** If two players both run ultra-greedy boss builds with weak wave clear, your run can collapse before the boss phase even starts.

In the **marvel rivals blood hunt meta**, balanced pace wins more sessions than pure theoretical DPS.
