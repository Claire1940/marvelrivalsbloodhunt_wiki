## The Foundations of the Blood Hunt Mode

Blood Hunt is a progressive PvE experience where players face escalating waves of enemies culminating in epic boss encounters. For Moon Knight, the gameplay loop involves using his Crescent Darts to clear groups of zombies while managing his Grappling Hook to avoid the red-telegraphed damage zones.

As you progress through Normal, Hard, and Extreme difficulties, you will unlock the Nightmare levels. Nightmare mode is the true test of your **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt moon knight build**, as enemies gain massive health pools and damage buffs. You earn gear drops from eliminating enemies, which are categorized into four main slots: Weapon, Armor, Accessory, and Exclusive. Each piece of gear has base stats and extended effects that vary based on rarity.

### Gear Rarity and Quality
When optimizing your build, you should prioritize higher-rarity items. Rare, Epic, and Legendary items not only provide higher stat values but also unlock additional "Extended Effects" that can fundamentally change how Moon Knight's abilities function.

> **💡 Tip:** Smelt your lower-level gear in the Forge menu to accumulate Uru shards. Use these shards to forge high-level gear specifically for Moon Knight to ensure your stats scale with the Nightmare difficulty.

## Optimal Traits for Moon Knight

The Trait system acts as a permanent skill tree for your hero. You earn one assignable point per level, up to the maximum level of 60. For a powerful **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt moon knight build**, you should focus on the columns that enhance his projectile speed and his health-on-hit capabilities.

Moon Knight’s trait tree is divided into General Traits and Ability-Specific Traits. In the Blood Hunt mode, survivability is just as important as DPS.

### Key Trait Selections

## Gear Optimization and Forging

The most critical part of your **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt moon knight build** is the Exclusive gear slot. Unlike general armor, Exclusive items are hero-specific and provide unique enhancements to Moon Knight's ultimate or his passive abilities. When forging at the Forge tab, you can select Moon Knight as your "Desired Hero" to guarantee the forged items are compatible with his kit.

### Best-in-Slot Gear for Moon Knight

1.  **Weapon (Moonlit Blades):** Look for the "Lifesteal" extended effect. This allows Moon Knight to recover health while dealing damage, reducing reliance on the three manual heal charges.
2.  **Armor (Vestments of the Night):** Prioritize armor that reduces the cooldown of mobility skills.
3.  **Accessory (Ankh of Life):** This item should ideally have "Increased Arcana Drop Rate" to help you scale faster within a single match.
4.  **Exclusive (Shroud of Khonshu):** The best version of this item grants Moon Knight "Unstoppable" status during his ultimate, preventing crowd control from Dracula or Kingpin.

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weapon** | Attack Power | **Lifesteal / Crit Rate** |
| **Armor** | Max Health | **Cooldown Reduction** |
| **Accessory** | Defense | **Movement Speed** |
| **Exclusive** | Ultimate Charge Rate | **Duration Increase** |

## Arcana and Match Scaling

Arcana points are earned during a match by clearing waves. When you open the Arcana store, you are presented with three random upgrades. For an effective **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt moon knight build**, you should focus on picking Arcana that synergizes with your existing gear.

If your gear focuses on critical hits, look for the "Deadly Aim" Arcana. If you are struggling with the boss phases, look for "Vampiric Touch." Additionally, don't forget the Arcane Realm menu outside of the match. This persistent level carries over across all heroes and provides permanent stat boosts like increased damage to bosses or faster movement speed.

> **Warning:** Be careful when choosing Arcana upgrades in the middle of a match. You only get one point per ring cleared, so picking a low-tier upgrade early can hamper your power level against the final boss.

## Surviving the Nightmare Bosses

Once you reach the higher Nightmare difficulties, the boss fights against Capwolf and Dracula become significantly more complex. Moon Knight's role is to maintain distance while dealing consistent damage.

### Boss Strategy Table

| Boss | Primary Threat | Moon Knight Counter-Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Capwolf** | High-speed lunges and melee swipes. | Use Grappling Hook to maintain a 15-meter distance; target the head for crit damage. |
| **Dracula** | Red "AOE" damage zones and life-drain. | Stay out of the red highlighted areas on the ground at all costs. Save the Ultimate for the "Blood Crystal" phase. |
| **Kingpin** | Heavy ground slams and summons. | Use Crescent Darts to clear the summoned minions first before focusing on Kingpin's shield. |

In the "Blood Crystal" phase (Phase 12), your goal isn't just to kill zombies. You must destroy the red blood crystals at the corners of the castle to break the magic circle. Use Moon Knight’s mobility to zip between the crystals while your teammates distract the horde.

If you are playing [Marvel Rivals](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2767030/Marvel_Rivals/) solo, remember that the enemies have less health, but you lose the benefit of team-based buffs. In solo play, your **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt moon knight build** must prioritize health regeneration and defensive traits to survive the relentless waves.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the Nightmare difficulties for Moon Knight?**

A: You must first complete the Normal, Hard, and Extreme difficulties. Once you finish Extreme mode by defeating Dracula, you will unlock Nightmare 1 through 5. Clearing higher Nightmare levels unlocks subsequent tiers (e.g., clearing Nightmare 5 unlocks 6-10).

**Q: What is the best way to get Uru shards for my Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt moon knight build?**

A: The most efficient way is to run Hard or Extreme difficulty matches and collect all gear drops. Go to the Forge menu and use the "Select All" feature to smelt any gear that is lower than your current hero level or rarity requirement.

**Q: Does the Arcane Realm level reset between heroes?**

A: No, the Arcane Realm level and the points you assign there are account-wide. This means any progress you make while playing Moon Knight will also benefit your other heroes, like Squirrel Girl or Punisher.

**Q: Which Exclusive gear is best for Moon Knight in Nightmare 100?**

A: For the highest difficulties, the "Silver Crescent" exclusive gear is highly recommended. It adds a ricochet effect to your primary fire, allowing you to hit multiple zombies with a single dart, which is essential for clearing the massive hordes in Nightmare 100.
