## Understanding Blood Hunt Difficulty Tiers

Before diving into the specifics of Thor's talents, it is vital to understand the environment you will be fighting in. Blood Hunt operates on a progressive difficulty system. Completing one tier unlocks the next, leading up to the infinite scaling of Nightmare rounds.

| Difficulty | Number of Waves | Key Objectives | Unlocks |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal** | 5 Waves | Introduction to horde mechanics | Hard Mode |
| **Hard** | 9 Waves | Increased enemy health pools | Extreme Mode |
| **Extreme** | 14 Waves | Secret boss on Wave 14 (if side objectives met) | Nightmare Mode |
| **Nightmare** | 5 Waves | Boss fight at the end of every run | Higher Gear Rarity (up to Level 120) |

> **Warning:** In Nightmare difficulty, team wipes result in an immediate game over unless you possess Revive Tokens. Thor is often the "anchor" of the team, so prioritize your defensive stats to prevent a full squad failure.

## The Core Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Thor Build: Rune Awakening

The most effective way to play Thor in the 2026 Blood Hunt meta is by focusing on the **Rune Awakening** ability. This build relies on a tight synergy between your talent points and your equipped gear. When optimized, Thor's basic attacks and hammer throws generate massive amounts of Odinforce, allowing you to stay in an empowered state almost indefinitely.

### Talent Tree Allocation
Thor’s skill tree is divided into general traits (left) and ability upgrades (right). To unlock advanced ability upgrades, you must first invest points into the corresponding attribute on the same row.

*   **Left Tree (General):** Prioritize Health and Odinforce regeneration. These are the foundations of your survivability.
*   **Right Tree (Ability):** Focus exclusively on Rune Awakening nodes. These upgrades increase the duration of your buffs and the damage of your lightning strikes.
*   **Ability Mutators:** Select the talent that allows your base attack to regain Odinforce. This ensures you are never "starved" for energy during long wave clears.

### Recommended Skill Priorities
1.  **Enhanced Fury:** Unlocked after investing in the Health attribute. This increases your damage output as your health fluctuates.
2.  **Enchanted Flurry:** A critical upgrade for Rune Awakening. This talent significantly boosts your attack speed, which scales directly with high-rarity gear bonuses.
3.  **Hammer Shield:** Mutates your hammer throw to circle around you as a shield. This provides 360-degree protection while you are in the thick of a vampire swarm.

## Arcana: Major and Minor Progression

Progression in Blood Hunt isn't just about Thor; it’s about your account-wide Arcana level. This system provides buffs that apply to every character you play, but for Thor, specific slots are more valuable than others.

### Major Arcana
These are permanent unlocks. As you level up, assign points to slots that boost physical resistance and ability damage. Note that the final two scrolls on the right side of the UI only unlock after reaching **Nightmare Level 70**, representing the true endgame for dedicated players.

### Minor Arcana (Round Buffs)
At the start of every round within a match, you are presented with three random buffs. For a Thor build, you should prioritize:
*   **Attack Speed Buffs:** Directly complements Enchanted Flurry.
*   **Life Steal:** Essential for maintaining high health without relying solely on Jeff or other healers.
*   **Cooldown Reduction:** Allows for more frequent use of your ultimate and shield abilities.

## Advanced Strategies for Nightmare Difficulty

Once you reach the Nightmare 50+ range, the difficulty spike becomes noticeable. Enemies have significantly higher health pools, and bosses like Dracula gain new attack patterns. 

1.  **Synergy Verification:** Always hover over your talents in the skill tree. If your gear matches the ability, you should see a bonus percentage (e.g., +59% Attack Speed). If this number is low, your gear is not optimized for your chosen talents.
2.  **The Lock Feature:** Use the "Lock" feature in your inventory to protect your best Epic and Legendary gear. Use the "Dismantle Unlocked" filter to clear out hundreds of blue and green items in seconds, keeping your Uru Shard reserves high.
3.  **Positioning:** As Thor, you should always be the first person to engage the wave. Your hammer shield mutator allows you to draw aggro safely while your teammates (like Punisher or Moon Knight) deal damage from the periphery.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best ability to build around for Thor in Blood Hunt?**

A: Currently, Rune Awakening is considered the strongest choice for a **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt thor build**. It offers the best balance of sustained DPS and survivability by synergizing with high attack speed gear.

**Q: How do I unlock the secret boss on Extreme difficulty?**

A: On Extreme mode, you must complete three side objectives that appear randomly during the first 13 waves. If successful, the secret boss will replace the standard ending or appear as a 14th wave.

**Q: Do my Arcana levels reset when I switch characters?**

A: No. Major Arcana is account-wide progression. However, Thor’s specific character level, talent tree, and gear are unique to him and must be leveled independently from other heroes like Blade or Squirrel Girl.

**Q: What should I do with Uru Shards in the 2026 season?**

A: Use Uru Shards at the Forge to craft gear for your highest-level character. The rarity of the crafted gear is based on your current level, so wait until you hit a plateau in Nightmare difficulty before spending a large amount of currency.
