## Understanding the Blood Hunt PvE Mechanics

The Blood Hunt mode is a significant upgrade over the previous "Zombies" mode, featuring a 97-day limited run in 2026. One of the most critical changes is that character leveling is no longer account-wide. If you want to play a high-level Magik, you must grind her individually to unlock higher-tier gear slots and trait points.

Characters now have a "Rating" based on their equipped gear. This gear can be found as drops from enemies or forged using the new Smelt and Forge system. For Magik, focusing on gear that boosts close-range damage and ultimate charge speed is vital, as her "Seven Seals" ultimate is her primary tool for clearing large groups of vampires.

### Key Gameplay Features in 2026
*   **Individual Leveling:** Each hero must be leveled separately to unlock trait points.
*   **Trait Trees:** A branching skill tree allows you to customize abilities (often choosing between two distinct playstyles).
*   **The Forge:** Scrap unwanted gear to create high-level items for your specific hero.
*   **Active Healing:** All heroes now have an active heal (Left D-Pad) with three charges that regenerate over time.

## Gear Forging and Stat Priorities

The Forge is where your **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt magik build** truly comes together. By smelting down common (green) and rare (blue) items, you gain shards that can be used to forge hero-specific gear. Since Magik is a melee-centric Duelist, your gear should prioritize stats that reward being in the thick of the fight.

### Recommended Gear Stats for Magik
1.  **Bonus Damage vs. Close-Ranged Enemies:** This is the single most important stat for Magik. Since she is always within melee distance, this provides a flat damage increase to every swing.
2.  **Total Output Boost:** A general multiplier that affects both your sword strikes and your Limbo summons.
3.  **Damage vs. Healthy Opponents:** Great for clearing out elite mobs quickly before they can chip away at your health.
4.  **Boss Damage:** Essential for the final encounter against the vampiric Captain America.

## Skill Tree & Trait Optimization

As you level up Magik, you will earn assignable points. These points can be reset at any time for free, so don't be afraid to experiment. However, for the Hard and Extreme difficulties of Blood Hunt, you need a specialized setup.

The **Ruler of Hel** (Gold) traits generally focus on Magik’s physical sword attacks. This is often superior in PvE because the AI enemies tend to cluster together, making her wide sword arcs incredibly efficient. The **Disciple of Limbo** (Blue) traits focus on her portals and Stepping Disc mobility. While useful in PvP, the mobility is less critical in PvE where the goal is to hold a point or burn down a boss.

### Optimal Trait Progression
*   **Level 1-5:** Focus on the **Total Damage** nodes. This ensures you can one-shot the basic vampire fodder.
*   **Level 6-10:** Invest in **Ultimate Charge**. In the Blood Hunt mode, your ultimate is your "get out of jail free" card.
*   **Level 11+:** Start specializing in the **Gold Path** to enhance your Soulsword's reach and damage.

## Recommended Scrolls and Arcana Cards

During a Blood Hunt run, you will encounter the Arcana Store. This allows you to pick temporary power-ups that last for the duration of the match. For a **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt magik build**, your choices should complement your aggressive playstyle.

**Top Tier Arcana Cards:**
*   **Boss Damage (30%):** This is non-negotiable for higher difficulties. It turns the Captain America fight from a 5-minute slog into a 60-second burst window.
*   **Ultimate Charge Speed:** Synergizes with your gear and traits to let you use Seven Seals in almost every encounter.
*   **Damage vs. Healthy Opponents:** Helps you initiate fights by instantly deleting the first few enemies you hit.

> **Warning:** Avoid cards that increase movement speed at the expense of damage. Magik already has high mobility through her Stepping Discs; more speed won't help you clear waves faster.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best way to level up Magik for the Blood Hunt mode?**

A: The fastest way to level is to run the "Normal" difficulty solo or with a group. Each run takes about 5 minutes and provides significant experience. Once you reach level 8 and have forged some decent gear, move to "Hard" for better drop rates.

**Q: Can I use the same Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt magik build for PvP?**

A: No. The traits and gear system are exclusive to the Blood Hunt PvE mode. In PvP, Magik relies on her base kit and team synergies, whereas in PvE, she relies on stat-stacking and trait modifiers to handle hundreds of enemies.

**Q: How do I unlock the "Kingpin Dethroned" accessory?**

A: You must defeat the hidden boss within the Blood Hunt mode and collect shards. Once you have enough shards, you can craft the accessory in the Forge menu. There is also a gold version available for players who collect all the necessary components.

**Q: Which difficulty should I farm for gear?**

A: If you are looking for specific gear pieces for your **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt magik build**, "Hard" difficulty is the sweet spot. It offers a much higher drop rate for blue and purple items compared to "Normal" without the extreme frustration of "Extreme" difficulty.
