Unlike the standard PvP matches, the PvE mode introduces a robust trait system that allows for massive increases in range and damage. When built correctly, Moon Knight’s Ankhs can bounce between multiple targets with "disgusting" range, effectively acting as an automated meat grinder for trash mobs.

Furthermore, his sustainability is unmatched when paired with specific lifesteal or close-quarters damage traits. As you progress from the early levels toward the level 70 cap, the damage output begins to scale exponentially, allowing Moon Knight to transition from a single-target assassin into a high-utility room clearer.

## Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Moon Knight: Essential Traits

To reach the legendary Nightmare 200 difficulty, you cannot rely on base stats alone. The trait system in Blood Hunt allows you to customize Moon Knight's kit. The current meta favors a "Blender" build, which focuses on maximizing the speed and frequency of projectile bounces.

### Top Tier Traits for Moon Knight

1.  **Ankh Range Extension:** Increases the distance projectiles travel between enemies. This is the core of the build.
2.  **Boss Hunter:** Provides a flat percentage damage increase against elite enemies and bosses like Dracula.
3.  **Close Quarters Specialist:** Grants a 20% damage boost when fighting within 5 meters, which is common during chaotic castle interior fights.
4.  **Assassination Protocol:** Increases critical hit damage when hitting enemies from behind or while they are distracted by teammates.

## Navigating the Blood Hunt Difficulty Tiers

Blood Hunt is not a static experience. The mode scales from basic introductory levels up to the daunting Nightmare 200. As the difficulty increases, enemy health pools become massive, and the "meat grinder" effect of Moon Knight becomes necessary to keep the team from being body-blocked by Dracula’s minions.

Players typically reach level 16 within the first few sectors, but the real challenge begins around level 50. At this stage, you must have a dedicated tank or a highly mobile team to survive the "Kill Box" rooms. While Moon Knight doesn't have the health of Thor or Hulk, his ability to "mog" enemies with high-speed projectiles keeps him safe.

### Key Difficulty Milestones

*   **Levels 1-20:** Focus on gathering basic damage traits and learning the map layout of Grand Central.
*   **Levels 21-50:** Enemies begin to use jump pads and AC130-style aerial attacks. Verticality becomes your best friend.
*   **Levels 51-70:** The level cap. Here, your build must be finalized to handle the endgame bosses.
*   **Nightmare 100+:** Every mistake is lethal. Moon Knight must use his mobility to stay on the edges of the arena.

## Boss Strategy: Defeating Dracula

The final confrontation in the Blood Hunt mode takes place at the top of the dark castle. Dracula is a formidable foe who uses "Dark Seals" and blood crystals to shield himself and summon endless waves of priests. 

When playing as Moon Knight, your job is two-fold:
1.  **Crystal Destruction:** Use your high burst damage to one-shot the blood crystals located around the arena. This breaks Dracula's invulnerability.
2.  **Dracula Focus:** Once the shields are down, activate your ultimate. With "Boss Hunter" traits, Moon Knight can delete Dracula's health bar in seconds, often referred to by the community as "bodying" the Vampire King.

> **Warning:** Do not ignore the vampire priests. If left unchecked, they will perma-spawn minions that can trap you in a corner, leading to a quick wipe in higher Nightmare tiers.

## Advanced Tips for Nightmare Runs

*   **Abuse Verticality:** Use jump pads to stay above the fray. If you stand on the edge of high platforms, ground-based minions will often path awkwardly below you, making them easy pickings.
*   **Loot Goblins:** Keep an eye out for the "Loot Fox" or Goblin enemies. They drop high-tier traits that are essential for late-game scaling.
*   **Ultimate Management:** Don't save your ultimate for just the boss. In Nightmare 200, the sheer volume of enemies often requires an ultimate just to clear a path to the next objective.
*   **Ankh Management:** Learn the bounce physics. Aiming at the central enemy in a cluster maximizes the chances of your projectiles hitting the maximum number of targets.
