## New Gameplay Mechanics: Loot and Progression

The Blood Hunt mode introduces a sophisticated loot system that was absent in earlier iterations. Each of the six playable heroes now has four dedicated gear slots. This adds a layer of RPG-style progression to the action-packed gameplay.

1.  **Gear Slots:** Players can find equipment specific to their hero. For example, Moon Knight can equip "Lunar Gauntlets" that provide up to a 78% damage bonus against bosses.
2.  **Trait Progression:** As you clear waves, you earn experience that unlocks temporary traits for that specific run, similar to a roguelike structure.
3.  **Card System:** The ability upgrade cards return from the Zombies mode but have been rebalanced to include elemental effects, such as Blade's fire-imbued shotgun.

> **Tip:** Focus on farming "Normal" difficulty to gear up your main hero before attempting "Nightmare" mode. The jump in enemy health is significant and requires optimized gear stats.

## Boss Encounters in Blood Hunt

The boss lineup for Season 7 is significantly more challenging than previous seasons, featuring double the boss count of the Zombies event. Each encounter requires a specific strategy to overcome, especially on higher difficulties.

### Boss Strategy Guide

| Boss | Primary Threat | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Capwolf** | High Melee Burst | Keep distance; use Luna Snow’s freeze to kite |
| **Ratatskr** | Summoning Swarms | Use Squirrel Girl’s AOE to clear minions quickly |
| **Kingpin** | Shielding/Armor | Use Moon Knight’s bonus boss damage gear to break armor |
| **Dracula** | Life Steal/Lunge | Blade’s fire abilities negate Dracula’s healing |

Dracula remains the final encounter and is a multi-phase fight. During the second phase, he will transform into a swarm of bats, becoming invulnerable. Players must focus on the environment-based "Sunlight Crystals" to force him back into human form.
