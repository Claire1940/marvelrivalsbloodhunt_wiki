## Understanding the Gear System in Blood Hunt

Before diving into the forge, you must understand what you are picking up during your runs. In the Blood Hunt mode, gear drops from defeated enemies and bosses like Capwolf and Dracula. Each hero has four distinct gear slots that define their combat capabilities:

1.  **Weapon:** Enhances primary and secondary fire modes.
2.  **Armor:** Boosts health, damage reduction, and survivability.
3.  **Accessory:** Provides utility and cooldown management.
4.  **Exclusive:** Hero-specific items that dramatically alter how certain abilities function.

Each piece of gear comes with a specific Level and Rarity. You cannot equip gear that is a higher level than your current Hero Level (which caps at 60). As rarity increases, so does the number of "Extended Effects"—unique passive bonuses that can change your playstyle.

## Step-by-Step: How to Smelt Gear in Marvel Rivals

The process of **how to smelt gear in marvel rivals** takes place entirely within the Forge menu, which is accessible from the Blood Hunt main lobby. Smelting is the act of destroying unwanted gear to receive Uru Shards, the primary currency used for crafting new, high-rarity equipment.

Follow these steps to clean out your inventory:

1.  **Navigate to the Forge Tab:** From the hero customization screen in Blood Hunt, select the "Forge" option at the top of the interface.
2.  **Enter the Smelting Menu:** Within the Forge, look for the "Smelt" sub-menu. This will display all the gear currently in your inventory that is not equipped on a hero.
3.  **Select Your Items:** You can manually click individual pieces of gear to add them to the smelting queue. You will see a preview of the total Uru Shards you will receive.
4.  **Use Filters for Efficiency:** To speed up the process, use the filter icon. You can set the filter to select all "Common" or "Uncommon" items automatically.
5.  **Confirm the Smelt:** Once you are satisfied with your selection, click the Smelt button. The items will be consumed, and the Uru Shards will be added to your account.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Smelting is permanent. Once an item is converted into Uru Shards, it cannot be recovered. Always "Lock" your favorite high-tier gear to prevent accidental smelting.

## Forging New Gear with Uru Shards

Once you have mastered **how to smelt gear in marvel rivals**, you will likely have a surplus of Uru Shards. These shards are used in the "Forge" section to create new gear for specific heroes. This is the most reliable way to obtain Epic and Legendary gear without relying solely on RNG drops from boss fights.

When forging, you can customize your output by clicking on the central shard icon. This allows you to choose:
*   **The Target Hero:** Ensure the gear is for the character you are currently maining.
*   **The Gear Slot:** If you specifically need a better "Exclusive" item, you can force the forge to produce only that category.
*   **The Level Range:** You can spend more shards to guarantee the gear matches your hero's current high level.

### Forging Probability and Rarity

The quality of the forged item is still subject to RNG. You can hover over the "Quality Details" icon in the forge menu to see the exact percentage chance of hitting each rarity level. Generally, the more shards you invest in a single forge attempt, the better your odds of receiving a high-tier item.

| Forge Type | Shard Cost | Legendary Chance | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Forge** | 100 | ~1% | Filling empty slots |
| **Advanced Forge** | 500 | ~5% | Hunting for Blue/Purple gear |
| **Elite Forge** | 2,500 | ~15% | End-game optimization |

## Optimizing Your Build for Nightmare Difficulties

The primary reason to learn **how to smelt gear in marvel rivals** is resource management for the Nightmare modes. As you progress from Nightmare 1 through Nightmare 100, the health and damage of zombies and bosses like Kingpin and Vampiric Fury scale exponentially. 

To survive, you need a "Synergy Build." This involves matching your Gear effects with your **Traits** and **Arcana**. For example, if you are playing Punisher, you might want gear that enhances his turret mode, paired with Traits that turn the turret into an automated sentry.

### Managing Your Inventory
*   **Lock Vital Gear:** Any gear with a "God Roll" (perfectly synergized extended effects) should be locked immediately.
*   **Share with Friends:** If you find a piece of gear that is perfect for a friend's main hero, use the "Share" option in the inventory to send it to them rather than smelting it.
*   **Daily Smelting:** Get into the habit of smelting your "trash" loot after every 2-3 runs to keep your inventory manageable and your shard count high.

> **💡 Tip:** Hover over specific stats like "Close-range Enemy" or "Healthy Enemy" in the gear menu to see the exact distance or health percentage required to trigger those bonuses.

## The Role of Arcana and Traits

While smelting gear provides the raw stats, the Arcana and Trait systems provide the mechanical depth. In every Blood Hunt match, you earn Arcana points to buy random upgrades. Outside of matches, you can level up your **Arcane Realm**, which provides permanent stat boosts that apply to all your heroes.

Traits are hero-specific skill trees. You earn one point per level up to level 60. These trees often have "Free Upgrades" next to general traits that don't cost points but significantly change how an ability works. Mastering **how to smelt gear in marvel rivals** ensures you have the base stats (Attack, Health, Armor) to make these Trait-based ability modifiers truly shine.

## FAQ

**Q: Does smelting gear give me back the gold spent on upgrades?**

A: No, smelting only returns Uru Shards. Any currency or materials spent on upgrading or modifying the gear are lost, so be certain before you decide to break down an item.

**Q: Can I smelt gear that is currently equipped on another hero?**

A: No. The smelting menu automatically hides any gear that is currently equipped. If you want to smelt a piece of equipment, you must first unequip it from the hero's gear screen.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many Uru Shards I can hold?**

A: As of the 2026 update, there is no known hard cap on Uru Shards. However, it is generally better to spend them on forging to improve your gear score rather than hoarding them.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get gear to smelt?**

A: Running Nightmare mode levels that you can clear quickly (usually around Nightmare 5-10) is the most efficient way to farm a high volume of gear. If you are struggling, disabling "Auto-fill Teammates" and playing solo will scale down the enemy health, often allowing for faster clears. Knowing **how to smelt gear in marvel rivals** effectively will help you turn these fast runs into high-tier legendary items.
