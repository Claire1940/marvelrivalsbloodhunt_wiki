Optimizing your hero's loadout is essential for surviving the higher difficulties of the Blood Hunt PvE mode. If you are wondering **how do you smelt gear in Marvel Rivals**, you have come to the right place. Understanding the mechanics of the forge and inventory management is a core skill for any player looking to clear Nightmare-level challenges. Learning **how do you smelt gear in Marvel Rivals** allows you to clear out your cluttered inventory and convert unwanted items into valuable Uru shards, which are necessary for crafting high-tier equipment that perfectly suits your chosen hero build.

## Understanding the Forge and Smelting Process

In the Blood Hunt mode, gear drops frequently as you defeat waves of enemies. Over time, your inventory will fill with items of varying levels and rarities. Instead of letting these items sit idle, the game provides a dedicated smelting system to help you progress. 

The smelting process is straightforward but requires careful management to ensure you do not destroy items you might need for future builds. When you navigate to the Forge menu, you gain access to all your currently held equipment that is not actively equipped on a hero.

### Why Smelt Your Gear?

| Benefit | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Uru Shards** | The primary currency gained from smelting; used to craft new, higher-quality gear. |
| **Inventory Space** | Frees up slots to collect more loot during your next PvE run. |
| **Build Optimization** | Allows you to discard low-level items to focus resources on specific stat enhancements. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Always check your locked items before performing a mass smelt. If you have a piece of gear with a unique effect that you plan to use later, use the "Lock" feature to prevent it from being accidentally converted into shards.

## Step-by-Step: How to Smelt Gear

To effectively manage your resources, follow these steps within the Forge menu:

1. **Navigate to the Forge Tab:** Access the menu from the main Blood Hunt interface.
2. **Review Inventory:** Select the gear category (Weapon, Armor, Accessory, or Exclusive) you wish to clear.
3. **Filter Items:** Use the built-in filters to identify gear based on rarity or level. This is helpful when you want to quickly select all low-level items for smelting.
4. **Select and Smelt:** Choose individual items or use the "Select All" function for non-locked gear. Confirm the smelting action to receive your Uru shards.

Once you have your shards, you can return to the Forge section to create new gear. Remember that you can click the center shard icon to select a specific hero and gear slot, ensuring your forged items align with your current character's needs.

## Maximizing Your Build with Uru Shards

Crafting is a game of probability. When you use your Uru shards at the forge, the rarity of the resulting gear is RNG-based. To get a better understanding of your odds, hover over the exclamation mark next to the "Quality Details" section in the forge menu. This will display the percentage chance for obtaining higher-tier gear, helping you decide when to save or spend your shards.

## Strategic Tips for Gear Management

*   **Prioritize Rarity:** Higher rarity gear provides not only better base stats but also additional extended effects that can drastically change how a hero plays.
*   **Level Requirements:** Keep in mind that equipment has level requirements. Do not hoard gear that is significantly below your current hero level; smelt it to fund the creation of gear that you can actually equip.
*   **Build Synergy:** Before smelting, ensure the gear does not have an effect that complements one of your hero's traits. Use the "Share" feature if you are unsure if a piece of gear is worth keeping—your friends might have better insight into specific hero builds.

## FAQ

**Q: Does smelting gear give back the same amount of shards regardless of level?**

A: No. The amount of Uru shards you receive is scaled based on the level and the rarity of the item being smelted. Higher-tier items yield significantly more shards.

**Q: How do I make sure I don't smelt my best gear?**

A: Use the "Lock" feature on any gear you intend to keep. The game’s "Select All" function will automatically ignore any items that are locked, keeping them safe from accidental conversion.

**Q: Is there a limit to how much gear I can smelt at once?**

A: You can select all your unlocked gear at once to smelt them. This is the most efficient way to clear your inventory after a long session of farming Blood Hunt.

**Q: When should I start smelting gear in Marvel Rivals?**

A: You should start using the smelting feature as soon as your inventory begins to fill up with low-level items that you no longer need. It is better to have Uru shards available for crafting than to have a full inventory of gear you cannot use.
