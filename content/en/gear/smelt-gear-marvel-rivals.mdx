In the Blood Hunt mode, each hero has four distinct gear slots: Weapon, Armor, Exclusive, and Accessory. Each item comes in five distinct rarities that determine its base power and potential for extended effects.

| Gear Rarity | Color Code | Drop Location | Forging Potential |

## How to Smelt Gear in Marvel Rivals

The smelting process is handled within the Forge menu. As you play through waves of vampires and zombies, gear drops as colored orbs. While these are automatically collected at the end of each wave, your inventory will quickly become cluttered with "Advanced" and "Normal" items that no longer serve your progression.

To **smelt gear marvel rivals** items effectively, follow these steps:

1.  Navigate to the **Forge** menu from the hero progression screen.
2.  Select the **Scrap/Smelt** tab.
3.  Use the **Filter** tool to highlight specific rarities (e.g., filter for all Advanced and Normal items).
4.  Review any items with unique Extended Effects before confirming.
5.  Select **Smelt All** to convert the items into Uru Shards.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Smelting is an irreversible process. Once you convert an item into Uru Shards, it cannot be recovered. Always double-check your "Exclusive" slot items, as these often provide hero-specific ability modifiers that are difficult to replace.

The amount of Uru Shards returned depends entirely on the rarity and level of the gear. For instance, smelting a full inventory of Advanced gear can yield upwards of 12,000 Uru Shards, which is enough to begin crafting higher-level replacements.

## Hero Traits and Arcana Progression

The gear system works in tandem with the Hero Trait tree and Arcana system. As you level up your hero by playing the Blood Hunt mode, you earn assignable points. These points are spent on a massive skill tree that upgrades base stats permanently. 

Unlike the gear system, which can be randomized, the Trait tree provides a linear and predictable path to power. You can choose between two main ability paths (such as Moon Knight's melee vs. ranged paths) which change how your primary abilities function. To supplement this, the **Arcana Realm** allows for minor stat boosts that you select during a match. While these aren't as impactful as your forged gear, they provide the final "boost" needed to survive the instant-kill mechanics of bosses like Dracula and Kingpin.

## Boss Encounters and Exclusive Rewards

The ultimate goal of the smelting and forging loop is to prepare for the four major boss encounters in the Blood Hunt update. Each boss requires a specific level of gear optimization.

1.  **Capwolf:** A high-mobility boss with dash attacks similar to Wolverine. Requires decent armor stats.
2.  **Ratatosca:** The easiest encounter, focusing on basic projectile dodging.
3.  **Dracula:** A massive difficulty spike. He summons blood swords and features an instant-kill explosion. High DPS is required to break his blood swords quickly.
4.  **Kingpin (Secret Boss):** Only accessible if you complete three hidden missions (keys) during the run. He features a "dimension" mechanic where your teammates become bots and attack you.

Defeating Kingpin rewards you with the **Kingpin Dethroned** accessory. To unlock the prestigious **Golden Kingpin Trophy**, you must collect 100 Trophy Shards, which drop randomly from Nightmare difficulty runs or as rewards for specific achievements.

## FAQ

**Q: Does smelting gear for one hero give Uru Shards that can be used for another?**

**Q: Can my teammates steal the gear orbs that drop during a match?**

A: No. Gear drops are client-side. The orbs you see on your screen are yours alone, and your teammates see their own unique drops. You never have to worry about "ninja looting" in this mode.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get Legendary gear?**

A: The most efficient method is to reach Nightmare level 65 or higher. At this stage, the drop rate for Legendary items increases, and the amount of shards you get from choosing to **smelt gear marvel rivals** items of lower rarity is significantly higher, allowing for more frequent high-tier crafting.

**Q: Is there a limit to how much gear I can smelt at once?**
