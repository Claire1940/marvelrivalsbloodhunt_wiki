## New Mechanics: Gear, Loot, and Traits

For the first time in Marvel Rivals, a comprehensive loot system has been integrated into the PVE experience. Each hero now possesses four dedicated gear slots. This gear is not merely cosmetic; it provides significant stat boosts and modifies how abilities function.

### The Four Gear Slots
1.  **Weapon Core:** Enhances primary fire damage (e.g., Blade’s shotgun fire rate).
2.  **Armor Plating:** Increases health and resistance to vampire "Bite" status effects.
3.  **Utility Charm:** Reduces cooldowns or increases the duration of buffs.
4.  **Hero Artifact:** Grants a unique trait, such as Moon Knight’s gauntlets providing 78% bonus damage against bosses.

### The Card System
Similar to the previous Zombies mode, the Card System returns with new "Vampiric" traits. After every encounter, players choose from three randomized cards. These can range from simple damage boosts to complex synergies, such as "Fiery Blood," which causes enemies to explode when killed by Blade’s burn effects.

## Boss Encounters and Strategies

The Blood Hunt mode features four major boss fights, doubling the count from previous PVE events. Each boss requires a specific strategy to overcome, especially on higher difficulties where one mistake can lead to a team wipe.

### 1. Capwolf (The Werewolf Captain)
A corrupted version of Captain America. He retains his shield but gains massive melee cleave damage.
*   **Strategy:** Keep your distance. Use Squirrel Girl’s summons to distract him while Blade applies burn stacks. When he prepares his "Howl" ability, hide behind environmental cover to avoid the fear debuff.

### 2. Ratatskr
The Asgardian Squirrel beast returns, but this time as a foe. She is incredibly fast and can burrow underground.
*   **Strategy:** Use Moon Knight’s ricochet darts to track her movement while she is near the surface. Luna Snow’s freeze abilities are vital for locking her down during her "Acorn Barrage" phase.

### 3. Kingpin (The Mayor of New York)
Wilson Fisk uses high-tech armor and calls in waves of human henchmen equipped with UV-stave weapons.
*   **Strategy:** Focus the adds first. Kingpin himself is slow; as long as the henchmen are cleared, Captain America can easily block his physical charges.

### 4. Dracula
The final boss of the Blood Hunt. He has three phases: Humanoid, Bat Swarm, and Ancient Vampire.
*   **Strategy:** In Phase 2, Dracula becomes invulnerable and splits into hundreds of bats. This is where Moon Knight and Squirrel Girl shine, as their AOE (Area of Effect) damage is the only way to force him back into his humanoid form.

> **Warning:** Dracula’s "Life Drain" grab cannot be blocked by shields. If a teammate is grabbed, all other players must focus fire on Dracula’s head to break the animation before the teammate is killed.

## Final Tips for Nightmare Difficulty

To earn the most prestigious **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt titles**, you must conquer Nightmare difficulty. This mode introduces "Vampire Elites" who can turn defeated players into thralls if they aren't revived within 10 seconds.

1.  **Communicate Cooldowns:** Never use Luna Snow’s ultimate and Squirrel Girl’s taunt at the same time. Rotate your "big" buttons to ensure you always have a safety net.
2.  **Farm Gear on Hard:** Do not jump straight into Nightmare. Spend time in Hard mode to find Epic-tier gear for your Weapon Core and Hero Artifact slots.
3.  **Target the Bats:** Flying vampire bats are the leading cause of wipes. They deal low damage but apply a stackable "Vulnerability" debuff that makes bosses hit 200% harder.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I equip the Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt titles I've earned?**

A: Go to the "Profile" tab in the main menu, select "Customization," and then click on "Titles." You can choose any unlocked title to display under your username.

**Q: Is Squirrel Girl a permanent addition to the roster?**

A: Yes, Squirrel Girl is a permanent addition to the Marvel Rivals roster as of Season 7, though her kit is specifically enhanced for the Blood Hunt PVE mode.

**Q: Can I earn Blood Hunt titles in solo play?**

A: While you can queue for Blood Hunt solo, you will be matched with other players. Currently, there is no "True Solo" mode that grants titles, as the difficulty scales specifically for a team of six.

**Q: Do the Hellfire Gala skins provide any bonuses in Blood Hunt?**

A: No, all skins are purely cosmetic. However, some skins feature unique VFX that can make certain abilities easier to track in the dark environments of the Blood Hunt maps.
