Dracula is not just a bullet sponge; he is a tactical nightmare. Based on the dialogue "I shall feast on your bones" and "You truly believe yourself my equal?", the fight appears to be a multi-phase struggle that tests your team's coordination.

| Phase | Dracula's Behavior | Recommended Tactics |

## Strategies for High-Difficulty Runs

When tackling the Blood Hunt on the highest settings, your margin for error is slim. The **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt trailer** hints at a law-and-order theme, with the final line mentioning "vigilante antics have violated the law." This might imply a secondary objective where players must avoid collateral damage or interact with local authorities while fighting the undead.

*   **Prioritize Elite Targets:** Don't get distracted by the small fry. If an elite vampire with a red aura appears, they must be eliminated immediately to prevent them from buffing the rest of the pack.
*   **Resource Management:** In PvE, health packs are scarce. Heroes with healing abilities must manage their cooldowns carefully to ensure the team is healthy before the final boss room.
*   **Environmental Kills:** New York has many ledges. Use knockback abilities to send vampires flying off the map to save ammunition and energy.
