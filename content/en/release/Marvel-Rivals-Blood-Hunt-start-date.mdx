## New PvE Mechanics: What Has Changed?

Since the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt start date**, the most immediate change players have noticed is the departure from account-wide PvE leveling. In previous iterations, leveling up one character would boost your entire roster. In Blood Hunt, progression is hero-specific. If you reach Level 8 with Moon Knight, your Punisher or Jeff the Night Shark will remain at Level 1 until you put in the work with them individually.

### The Survival Heal
A massive quality-of-life improvement introduced on the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt start date** is the dedicated heal ability. By pressing Left on the D-pad (or the corresponding keybind on PC), heroes can now self-heal up to three times. These charges regenerate over time, drastically reducing the reliance on specific support characters for solo or duo runs.

## The Gear and Forging System

One of the deepest additions available since the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt start date** is the gear system. Enemies now drop equipment that provides stat boosts, such as increased damage against close-range foes or faster ultimate charge rates.

### Smelting and Forging
If you find yourself with a surplus of low-level gear, you can use the "Smelt" function to break items down into Shards. These Shards are then used in the Forge to create high-level equipment tailored to your specific hero.

| Gear Action | Resource Required | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Smelting** | Common/Rare Gear | Shards (Currency) |
| **Forging (Level 1)** | 100 Shards | Random Level 1 Gear |
| **Forging (Level 8)** | 1,000 Shards | Random Level 8 Gear |
| **Direct Purchase** | 100 Units | **100,000 Shards** |

> **💡 Tip:** For a limited time, you can "cheat" the grind by purchasing 100,000 Shards for just 100 Units in the store. This allows you to forge high-level gear immediately, bypassing the early-game struggle.

## Character Traits and Skill Trees

Each hero in Blood Hunt features a branching skill tree. These traits can fundamentally change how a character plays. For example, Moon Knight has two primary paths:

1.  **Blue Path (Fist of the Moon God):** Enhances his traditional crescent darts and enks.
2.  **Gold Path (Phases of the Moon):** Replaces his darts with powerful melee-focused fist attacks.

It is important to note that these upgrades are often mutually exclusive. If you upgrade a trait that replaces an ability, you lose access to the original version. However, skill points can be reset at any time, allowing for experimentation as you climb the difficulty ladder.

## Boss Strategies: From Cap to Kingpin

The combat loop in Blood Hunt culminates in intense boss encounters. On Normal difficulty, your primary target is a corrupted Captain America.

### Defeating Captain America (Normal)
The fight takes place at the end of the Grand Central Station level. Cap uses his shield for both defense and high-damage ricochet attacks. 
*   **Phase 1:** Focus on clearing the minor mobs that spawn to keep the arena clear.
*   **Phase 2:** When Cap raises his shield, stop attacking to avoid reflect damage.
*   **Phase 3:** Use the Arcana Store buffs (purchased during the mission) to increase boss damage.

### The Hidden Boss: Kingpin
For those seeking the ultimate challenge, collecting specific shards throughout the Extreme difficulty missions can trigger a hidden encounter with Kingpin. Defeating him is the only way to obtain the "Kingpin Dethroned" gold accessory, which is currently the most sought-after cosmetic in the game.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the official Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt start date?**

A: The **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt start date** was officially April 25, 2026. The event is scheduled to run for 97 days from this date, though it may be extended.

**Q: Can I play Blood Hunt solo, or do I need a team?**

A: While Blood Hunt supports matchmaking, the Normal difficulty is easily soloable thanks to the new D-pad healing mechanic. However, Hard and Extreme difficulties are highly recommended for groups of three.

**Q: Does my progress carry over between different heroes?**

A: No. Unlike the previous zombie mode, each hero has their own individual level and gear rating. You must level up each character separately to unlock their specific titles and traits.

**Q: How do I unlock the gold Kingpin accessory?**

A: You must play on higher difficulties to collect "Kingpin Dethroned" shards. Once you have enough shards, you can craft the gold version of the accessory in the crafting menu.
