The limited-time Blood Hunt PVE event has introduced a layer of mystery and high-stakes challenge to the superhero brawler. While many players are content with completing the standard mission path, the most dedicated fans are hunting for the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Kingpin** secret boss encounter. This hidden fight is tucked away within the Extreme difficulty setting, requiring players to complete specific, timed objectives that are easy to miss during the chaos of battle. Mastering the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Kingpin** encounter is not just about bragging rights; it is the primary method for unlocking exclusive rewards like the Kingpin Plush accessory. In this guide, we will break down every secret key location, the best hero builds for solo or group play, and the mechanical nuances required to survive Wilson Fisk’s devastating "Kamehameha" waves and phantom clones.

## Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Kingpin: Secret Objective Locations

To face the Kingpin, you must collect three hidden "keys" by completing secret objectives throughout the mission. If you miss even one, the mission will conclude after the Dracula fight, skipping the Time Square finale entirely.

### Key 1: The Bridge Barrier
Early in the mission, you will encounter a large energy barrier guarded by a squad of enemies. To secure the first key, you must destroy this barrier almost instantly.
*   **Strategy:** Ignore the minor enemies. Use Moon Knight’s melee kick or high-damage ultimates to one-shot the barrier. If you move fast enough, the game will register the first secret objective as complete.

### Key 2: The Blood Clot Bush
Located in the Central Park section, this objective requires you to hunt down a specific environmental object known as a "Blood Plant" or "Blood Clot Bush."
*   **Location:** The spawn point is semi-randomized within the lower half of the park area. Look for a glowing red, pulsating plant.
*   **Scaling:** If playing solo, you only need to destroy one plant. In a full group, you may need to find and destroy up to three within the time limit.

### Key 3: The Castle Crystals
The final key is located on the third level of the castle, just before the Dracula encounter. This is a high-pressure timed event.
*   **Objective:** Destroy all glowing crystals that spawn around the arena.
*   **Strategy:** Do not focus on the enemies. Every time you destroy a crystal, it triggers a shockwave that clears nearby adds. Focus entirely on the crystals to stay within the timer shown at the top of the HUD.

## Navigating the Mid-Bosses

Even with the keys in hand, you must bypass several formidable bosses before reaching the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Kingpin**. Each of these bosses serves as a gear check for the final encounter.

1.  **Captain America:** You have a 3-minute window to defeat him before he enters an "Enraged" state. Stick close and use melee interrupts to prevent his shield slams.
2.  **Ratataska:** A relatively straightforward fight. Use cards that grant bonus damage to bosses. Watch for the golden "Crawler" enemies that spawn nearby; killing them provides extra gear upgrades mid-mission.
3.  **Dracula:** This fight has two distinct phases. In the second phase, Dracula will vanish, spawning five swords around the arena. You must destroy all five swords quickly to force him back into the fight.

## The Kingpin Boss Fight Strategy

Once Dracula is defeated, if you have all three keys, the game will transition you to Time Square. Kingpin has a massive 3,000 HP pool (scaling with player count) and several mechanics that can result in an instant wipe.

### Phase 1: The King of New York
Kingpin starts the fight with standard heavy melee attacks. However, he will frequently charge up a massive energy wave.
*   **The "Kamehameha" Wave:** This is his most dangerous move. It deals massive damage and can one-shot heroes with low armor. When you see the purple glow, dash behind him or use a movement ability to get out of the line of fire.

### Phase 2: Phantom Clones
At roughly 60% HP, Kingpin will summon a phantom clone of your hero. 
*   **Counter:** You must prioritize killing your own clone. The clone uses your same abilities and can stun you, making you vulnerable to Kingpin’s follow-up attacks.

### Phase 3: The Enrage Bubble
When Kingpin’s health drops below 30%, he enters his final state. He will generate a massive energy bubble that covers a large portion of the arena.
*   **The Bubble Mechanic:** You **must** stand inside the bubble to deal damage to him. If you are outside the bubble when he detonates his energy, you will be instantly eliminated. This is similar to the mechanics found in previous high-level PVE raids.

| Attack Name | Visual Cue | How to Counter |
|---|---|---|
| **Energy Wave** | Purple glow/charging stance | Dash behind him or to the side. |
| **Phantom Summon** | Dark portal under the player | Focus fire on the clone immediately. |
| **Overdrive Bubble** | Large white/blue dome | Enter the dome and stay close to Kingpin. |
| **Ground Slam** | Red circle on the floor | Jump or move out of the radius. |

## Rewards for Completion

Successfully defeating the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Kingpin** on Extreme difficulty rewards players with the **Kingpin Plush** accessory. This item can be equipped on any hero currently available in the PVE roster, including Moon Knight, Punisher, and Squirrel Girl.

For those looking for the ultimate challenge, completing this fight on Nightmare 100 difficulty (an even higher tier unlocked after Extreme) provides a chance to earn the **Golden Kingpin** accessory. Note that the secret objectives must be completed on Extreme specifically to trigger the initial unlock.

For more information on upcoming PVE events and hero balance changes, visit the [official Marvel Rivals site](https://www.marvelrivals.com/) for the latest developer updates.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I have to play as Moon Knight to unlock the Kingpin fight?**

A: No, you can use any hero. However, Moon Knight is highly recommended due to his ability to complete the "Bridge Barrier" secret objective very quickly, which is the easiest one to fail.

**Q: Can I unlock the Kingpin accessory on Normal difficulty?**

A: No, the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Kingpin** secret ending and the associated Kingpin Plush accessory are exclusive to the Extreme difficulty and higher.

**Q: What happens if I miss a secret key?**

A: If you miss a key, the mission will end immediately after the Dracula boss fight. You will receive the standard rewards for the mission, but you will not be transported to Time Square to fight Kingpin.

**Q: Is the Kingpin fight available in the permanent game modes?**

A: Currently, the Kingpin encounter is tied to the Blood Hunt PVE event. While some bosses return in future rotations, it is best to complete this now during the 2026 event window to ensure you get the exclusive accessories.
