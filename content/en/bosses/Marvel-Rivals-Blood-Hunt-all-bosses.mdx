## Encounter 1: The Wolf of the Monster Kingdom

The first major hurdle in your journey is The Wolf. This beast serves as the vanguard of Dracula’s army and focuses heavily on aggressive, high-mobility strikes. 

### Strategy and Tactics
The Wolf utilizes a "Bleed" mechanic that stacks damage over time. Healer heroes must prioritize cleansing these stacks or using burst healing when the Wolf performs his "Ancestral Sword Strike." 

1.  **Dodge the Pounce:** When the Wolf crouches, he is about to leap at the furthest player. 
2.  **Clear the Pack:** At 50% HP, he will summon smaller subordinates. Use Area of Effect (AoE) abilities to clear them quickly so they don't overwhelm your supports.
3.  **Interrupt the Howl:** If the Wolf successfully finishes his howl animation, his damage increases by 25% for the remainder of the phase.

> **💡 Tip:** Use heroes with knockback abilities to keep the Wolf away from your backline during his frenzied state.

## Encounter 2: Taskmaster (The Norn Stone Guardian)

Taskmaster returns as a formidable mid-game boss, tasked with guarding a stolen Norn Stone. His fight is unique because he adapts to the heroes you have selected. If you have a Captain America on your team, Taskmaster will utilize shield-based counters; if you have Iron Man, expect anti-air missiles.

### Key Mechanics
Taskmaster’s "Vigilante Antics" allow him to read your rights—literally. He will shout "I must read you your rights" before initiating a series of unblockable strikes.

*   **Secret Strike:** A high-speed dash that targets the player with the lowest health.
*   **Ability Mimicry:** He can temporarily disable one of your team's ultimate abilities.
*   **Tactical Retreat:** At 30% health, he uses smoke bombs to reposition, requiring players to find him before he regenerates armor.

## Encounter 3: Dracula, The Vampire King

Dracula is the centerpiece of the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt all bosses** roster. This fight is a multi-phase marathon that tests your team's endurance and resource management. Dracula views himself as the architect of a new eternal empire, and his dialogue reflects his god complex.

### Phase 1: The Crimson Chemist
In the first phase, Dracula fights alongside his "Crimson Chemist" subordinates. These alchemists throw vials of corrupted blood that create "Sealed in Blood" zones on the floor. Standing in these zones prevents healing.

### Phase 2: Rebirth and the Blood-Forged Sword
Once Dracula reaches 60% HP, he transforms. He draws his Blood-Forged Sword and begins using "Ancestral Sword, Strike!" which sends out waves of crimson energy.

| Ability | Effect | Counter |
|---|---|---|
| **Crimson Wave** | Massive AoE damage in a cone | Move behind Dracula during wind-up |
| **Blood Feast** | Dracula heals for 10% of damage dealt | Use shields to mitigate incoming hits |
| **Immortal Reign** | Summons a shield that must be broken | Focus all DPS on the shield immediately |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not let Dracula's "Blood Feast" trigger more than twice, or the fight will likely hit the enrage timer, leading to a team wipe.

## Encounter 4: The Final Showdown – The Mayor

In a shocking twist for the 2026 campaign, the final boss is revealed to be The Mayor (Norman Osborn), who has harnessed the power of the Norn Stone and potentially the legendary Excalibur. This fight takes place in the heart of New York City and represents the ultimate test for players hunting **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt all bosses**.

### The "Unlimited Power" Phase
The Mayor does not fight with traditional weapons but with cosmic energy. He will frequently shout "Unlimited Power!" before unleashing a screen-wide blast. 

1.  **Excalibur Strike:** The Mayor summons a spectral sword that tracks a single player. This player must kite the sword away from the team.
2.  **Justice Protocol:** He will attempt to "Read you your rights," which stuns players in a large radius.
3.  **Hollow Victory:** As his health nears zero, he will attempt a final self-destruct move. Players must deal a specific amount of burst damage within 10 seconds to "Seal" him away and save the city.

## Best Team Composition for Blood Hunt PvE

Beating the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt all bosses** requires a balanced team. While high DPS is tempting, sustain is the real winner in long boss fights.

*   **Tank (Vanguard):** Captain America or Thor. Their ability to mitigate large bursts of damage is vital during Dracula's sword phases.
*   **DPS (Duelist/Strategist):** Black Panther or Iron Man. High mobility is required to escape the "Sealed in Blood" zones.
*   **Support:** Luna Snow or Mantis. Consistent healing and the ability to cleanse debuffs like "Bleed" are mandatory for the Wolf and Dracula encounters.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock the secret Mayor boss in Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt?**

A: To face the Mayor, you must collect all three Norn Stone fragments dropped by Taskmaster and Dracula during a single run on Hard difficulty or higher. Beating the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt all bosses** in this specific order triggers the final New York City stage.

**Q: What is the best way to avoid Dracula's life steal?**

A: Dracula heals based on the damage he deals to "unshielded" targets. Bringing a hero like Magneto or Doctor Strange who can provide team-wide barriers will significantly reduce the amount of health Dracula recovers during the second phase.

**Q: Can I solo the Blood Hunt bosses?**

A: While the lower difficulty settings allow for solo play with AI companions, the higher tiers (Master and Grandmaster) are designed for a coordinated team of four players. Soloing the final boss, The Mayor, is extremely difficult due to the "Excalibur Strike" mechanic which requires one player to kite while others deal damage.

**Q: What rewards do I get for defeating all bosses?**

A: Completing the "Blood Hunt" gauntlet rewards players with the "Crimson Chemist" title, a unique skin for the hero who dealt the finishing blow to Dracula, and currency used to unlock 2026 seasonal gear.
