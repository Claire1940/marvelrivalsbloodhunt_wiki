## Combat Strategies and Tactics
When fighting the **Marvel Rivals Blood Hunt Capwolf**, mobility is your greatest asset. The boss relies on closing gaps quickly. If you are playing a character with low mobility, ensure you are positioned near a teammate who can provide shielding or crowd control.

### Effective Ability Usage
* **Save Ultimates:** Do not waste your ultimate abilities during Phase 1. Hold them for Phase 3 when the boss is most vulnerable to sustained burst damage.
* **Environmental Awareness:** Listen for voice cues and visual indicators regarding the "Heat" buildup. When the screen begins to glow, prioritize movement over damage output.
* **Synergy:** Coordinate with your team to chain stuns during the boss's recovery frames after his heavy slam attacks.
